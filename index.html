Graceful Living Resilient Foundations

Grace Resilviaa is a low rise residential enclave in Sector 78, Gurgaon, where thoughtful design, premium craftsmanship, and cosmic inspiration converge. Four story buildings with only two homes per floor ensure privacy and exclusivity.

About the

Location

Nestled at the foothills of the ancient Aravalli in Sector 78, Gurgaon, Grace Resilviaa offers a harmonious blend of urban convenience and natural tranquility. With seamless connectivity to major highways and business hubs, your weekdays begin with ease, while weekends invite you to unwind amidst expansive green landscapes and premier leisure destinations—just moments from home.
